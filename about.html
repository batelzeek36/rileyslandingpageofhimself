Riley Brown

About

Background

Riley Brown is a leading AI educator and content creator based in the Greater Seattle Area, recognized for his innovative approach to artificial intelligence and its applications across various domains. As someone who has never written a line of traditional code, Riley has revolutionized the way people think about app development through AI.

Expertise

Riley is the #1 AI educator on TikTok and has established a significant presence on YouTube, where he offers tutorials on building applications with AI, discussions on AI advancements, and demonstrations of AI tools. His expertise spans across multiple AI development platforms including Cursor, Claude, OpenAI, and various other modern AI tools.

Philosophy

Riley's approach centers around making AI development accessible to everyone. He believes that "one of the reasons why people don't get deep into computer science is they don't get hit with the 'aha' moment soon enough." Through his comprehensive guides and tutorials, he aims to provide that crucial moment of understanding for his audience.

Content Creation

His videos range from 8-minute quick builds to hour-long deep dives on AI development. You'll see him build and launch complete applications from start to finish, often in one video, demonstrating the power of AI-assisted development. His comprehensive guide to AI features over 100 practical applications, from basic chat tools to advanced coding implementations.

Impact

Through his educational content, Riley has influenced thousands of creators and entrepreneurs to explore AI development. His work spans multiple platforms where he shares real-time AI tips and tools, engaging a broad audience interested in the latest AI developments.
